# Opening & closing cameras | Mirasys Help Center

Source: https://documentation.mirasys.com/spotter-web-guide/V9.9/opening-closing-cameras

Opening & closing cameras

Select the amount of the grids or use Automatic

Opening cameras

Left mouse click top of the camera name in the device tree

Selected cameras are opened with the camera name and time stamp.

Closing cameras

Click the X icon from the needed camera image upper right corner
