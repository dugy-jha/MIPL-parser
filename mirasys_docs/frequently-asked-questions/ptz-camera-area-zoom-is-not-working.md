PTZ camera area zoom is not working

If PTZ camera area zoom does not work, then the reason is that the camera driver does not support the area zoom feature.

Below drivers are supporting area zoom:

NewAxisIPCapture

EHIIPCapture

CanonIPCapture

DahuaIPCapture

NewMobotixIPCapture

NewPanasonicIPCapture

NewSamsungIPCapture

PelcoIPCapture

NewSonyIPCapture

WisenetIPCapture
