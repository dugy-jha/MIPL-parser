V7 EOL and EOS
V7 EOL notification

Effective on March 31st, 2021, Mirasys is announcing the End of Life or EOL of Mirasys VMS V7.

V7 EOS notification

Effective on March 31st, 2022, Mirasys is announcing the End of Support or EOS of Mirasys VMS V7. Mirasys reserves the right to discontinue providing support for the VMS V7 license on 31/03/2022. Mirasys Customer Service may consider providing support assistance for End of Support Software at an agreed-upon fee.
