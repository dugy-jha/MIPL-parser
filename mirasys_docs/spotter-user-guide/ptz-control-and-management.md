PTZ Control and Management
Enabling PTZ Control

Open needed camera to the real-time view

Open camera toolbar

Click Camera and select PTZ control

Controlling PTZ camera

PTZ camera can be controlled with the mouse left button and dragging in the needed direction or with keyboard arrow keys.

Settings

The PTZ settings menu contains iris and focuses slider controls and editing the camera home position.The home position can be selected from the currently saved presets or tours.It is also possible to define how long the camera takes until it returns to the home position and if the switch to the home position is made only if it is not in some other tour or preset position.

Zoom

The zoom settings menu allows controlling the camera zoom. The camera zoom can also be controlled from the keyboard or the mouse wheel.
