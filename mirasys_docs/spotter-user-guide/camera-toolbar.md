Camera Toolbar

The camera toolbar is displayed when the mouse is moved over a camera or if a camera is selected with other means.If the mouse is not moved for some time, the camera toolbar disappears automatically.

The camera toolbar can contain the following items:

Settings

Export

Print

Camera

Dome control, duplicate modes

360 camera de-warping

Two-way audio

Image controls

Highlight

View or virtual zoom
