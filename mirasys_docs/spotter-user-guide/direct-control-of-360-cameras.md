Direct Control of 360 Cameras

When 360 de-warping is in use, it is possible to click on top of the image and then move the mouse and use the mouse wheel to zoom. This will act as an ultra-fast virtual PTZ. When clicking again with the mouse, the control is released and the changes saved.
