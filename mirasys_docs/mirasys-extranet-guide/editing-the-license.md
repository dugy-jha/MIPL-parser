Editing the license

The users can edit their licenses using the EDIT tab.

The default option is adding camera channels.

Select the license from the list

Click EDIT

From the right side, you can see detailed information about the license

From the camera channel box, you can see the existing amount of channels

Next, select a new total amount of camera channels

Below the camera channel box, you can see the total amount of camera channels in the stock

Enter all needed information to the details field

Click SAVE

Confirm stock reduction

Download updated license and send it to the installer
